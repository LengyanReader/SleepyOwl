Winter Views Around Linköping
@ Yunan | Sunday, Jan 31, 2021 | 1 minutes read | Update at Sunday, Jan 31, 2021

I’ve been here in Linköping for not a short time, it is small place, even its central city zone is just like a big country side place in China. For some reason, I came to this place. To some extent, I enjoy the relatively peaceful atmosphere here. Due to the covid-19’s influence, many good places can’t be visited. What I’ve done mostly these days is merely walking. Walking is not bad, during slow walking time, I took the good chance to feel and sense the fresh air here as well as enjoy the simple and cool views I met. .

Below are photos I took using my old phone, so the quality is hard to be ensure, please don’t laugh at my dumb photographing level.
